Restaurants

Blue Bayou Restaurant: Is located within the Pirates of the Caribbean attraction in New Orleans Square, the Blue Bayou Restaurant offers a unique dining experience with its atmospheric setting. Guests are seated in an outdoor courtyard overlooking the waters of the Pirates of the Caribbean ride. The menu features a variety of Cajun and Creole-inspired dishes. It is a favorite among Disneyland visitors seeking both flavorable cuisine and a touch of adventure.

Carnation Café: Located on Main Street, U.S.A., the Carnation Café is filled with classic American charm. The outdoor seating provides a perfect view of Main Street. This restaurant offers a menu filled with comfort food favorites. From Mickey Mouse-shaped waffles for breakfast to sandwiches and salads for lunch, Carnation Café offers a variety of options. The outdoor dining on Main Street and the delicious food make it a popular spot among guests.

Rancho del Zocalo Restaurante: Located in Frontierland, Rancho del Zocalo Restaurante, is a vibrant Mexican-inspired plaza. The restaurant is decorated with colorful tilework and is always playing lively music. The menu features a selection of Mexican-inspired dishes, such as enchiladas, tacos, and fajitas. Guests can enjoy their meals in the shaded outdoor seating area with views of the Rivers of America and Big Thunder Mountain Railroad.
