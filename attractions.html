Attractions

Space Mountain: Space Mountain, nestled in the futuristic landscape of Tomorrowland, is a high-speed indoor roller coaster that catapults riders into the depths of outer space. With its thrilling twists and turns in near darkness, the attraction combines the excitement of a roller coaster with the awe-inspiring illusion of interstellar travel. The experience is heightened by the mesmerizing soundtrack and the illusion of stars whizzing by, creating an otherworldly adventure that has captivated Disneyland visitors since its opening in 1977.

Pirates of the Caribbean: Embark on a swashbuckling journey through the Caribbean on the classic attraction, Pirates of the Caribbean. Located in New Orleans Square, this immersive ride takes visitors on a boat voyage through pirate-infested waters, where animatronic buccaneers engage in lively escapades. From the iconic theme song "Yo Ho (A Pirate's Life for Me)" to the intricate detailing of the scenes, including a town in the midst of pillaging, this timeless attraction, which opened in 1967, continues to be a favorite for guests of all ages.

Haunted Mansion: In the shadowy confines of New Orleans Square, the Haunted Mansion beckons brave souls to enter its ghostly abode. This dark ride combines spooky ambiance with lighthearted humor as visitors glide through a haunted estate filled with ghostly apparitions and eerie effects. Since its debut in 1969, the Haunted Mansion has charmed Disneyland guests with its clever storytelling and innovative use of audio-animatronics, making it a must-visit attraction for those seeking a delightful fright.
