Attractions

Space Mountain: Space Mountain, located in Tomorrowland, a futuristic landscape, is a high-speed indoor roller coaster that sends riders into outer space. With its thrilling twists and dark atmosphere, the attraction combines the excitement of a roller coaster with the illusion of outer space travel. The experience is also heightened by the unique soundtrack and the illusion of stars, creating an out-of-the-world adventure that has captivated Disneyland visitors since its opening in 1977.

Pirates of the Caribbean: Located in New Orleans Square, the Pirates of the Caribbean ride takes visitors on a boat voyage through the Caribbean. There are various pirate animatronics that role play different scenes in the “Pirates of the Caribbean” movie. Since its opening in 1967, this entertaining attraction continues to be a favorite among guests of all ages.

Haunted Mansion: Haunted Mansion: Located in New Orleans Square, the Haunted Mansion is a dark, slow-moving ride with mild scares. Visitors glide through a haunted mansion filled with ghost animatronics and eerie effects. The ride has become a must-visit attraction for many Disneyland guests since its debut in 1969.
